About us

The goal of this website is to teach those who are interested in learning to code the fundamentals of the ten most in-demand programming languages. Almost every program is made up of the same basic 'building blocks,' which are assembled in a variety of ways to achieve a certain goal. Variables, data types, sequence, selection, and iteration are just a few of the basic principles that all new programmers should be familiar with.

My intended audience is new programmers, students, and people from many disciplines who are interested in programming, and those who haven't made up their minds about their career options are all welcome. There will be no limit on experience level, as this will be just something to show people the basics of programming.
